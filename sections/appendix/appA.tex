\subsection{Proofs}\label{app:A}

\subsubsection{Proof: Recall Added 100\% = 
	Precision Closed 
	100\%}\label{app:A:RecallPrecision}
Proof of Recall added = Precision Closed 
when both are at 100\%:
\[
\textbf{Claim:}\quad 
\operatorname{Recall}_{\text{Added}} = 1 
\iff 
\operatorname{Precision}_{\text{Closed}} = 
1.
\]

\[
\textbf{Setup:}
\]
Consider a binary classification problem 
with classes 
\(\{\text{Added},\text{Closed}\}\).
Let "Added" be the positive class and 
"Closed" be the negative class, and define 
the confusion matrix counts:

\[
\begin{array}{c|cc}
	& \widehat{\text{Added}} & 
	\widehat{\text{Closed}} \\
	\hline
	\text{Added} & TP & FN \\
	\text{Closed} & FP & TN
\end{array}
\]

The recall for the class Added is
\[
\operatorname{Recall}_{\text{Added}} \;=\; 
\frac{TP}{TP+FN}.
\]

The precision for the class Closed 
(treating Closed as the class of interest) 
is
\[
\operatorname{Precision}_{\text{Closed}} 
\;=\; \frac{TN}{TN+FN}.
\]
(Here, the denominator \(TN+FN\) counts 
all predictions of 
\(\widehat{\text{Closed}}\), of which 
\(TN\) are correct and \(FN\) are 
incorrect.)

\[
\textbf{Proof:}
\]

\[
(\Rightarrow)\quad
\operatorname{Recall}_{\text{Added}} = 1
\;\Longrightarrow\;
\frac{TP}{TP+FN} = 1
\;\Longrightarrow\;
TP = TP+FN
\;\Longrightarrow\;
FN = 0.
\]

If \(FN=0\), then
\[
\operatorname{Precision}_{\text{Closed}}
= \frac{TN}{TN+FN}
= \frac{TN}{TN+0}
= 1.
\]
Hence 
\(\operatorname{Recall}_{\text{Added}} = 1 
\Rightarrow 
\operatorname{Precision}_{\text{Closed}} = 
1.\)

\[
(\Leftarrow)\quad
\operatorname{Precision}_{\text{Closed}} = 
1
\;\Longrightarrow\;
\frac{TN}{TN+FN} = 1
\;\Longrightarrow\;
TN = TN+FN
\;\Longrightarrow\;
FN = 0.
\]

If \(FN=0\), then
\[
\operatorname{Recall}_{\text{Added}}
= \frac{TP}{TP+FN}
= \frac{TP}{TP+0}
= 1.
\]
Hence 
\(\operatorname{Precision}_{\text{Closed}} 
= 1 \Rightarrow 
\operatorname{Recall}_{\text{Added}} = 1.\)

\[
\textbf{Conclusion:}\quad
\operatorname{Recall}_{\text{Added}} = 1 
\iff FN = 0 \iff 
\operatorname{Precision}_{\text{Closed}} = 
1.
\qquad\square
\]
